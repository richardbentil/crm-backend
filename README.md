# CRM API

# CRM User Manual

## Introduction

Welcome to the CRM system! This system helps you manage your contacts, tasks, sales pipelines, and more. You can also track emails, view reports, and stay organized with task reminders.

## How to Use the CRM

### 1. Manage Contacts

- **Add a Contact**: Click on the "Add Contact" button in the "Contacts" section, and fill out the form with the contact's details (name, email, phone, etc.).
- **Edit a Contact**: Click on any contact in the list to view and edit its details.
- **Search Contacts**: Use the search bar to quickly find contacts by name, email, or phone.

### 2. Manage Tasks

- **Create a Task**: In the "Tasks" section, click "Add Task" and fill in the task's details (title, assigned user, due date, etc.).
- **Edit a Task**: Click on a task to edit its details or mark it as completed.
- **Set Reminders**: You can set reminders for tasks, which will notify you when the due date is approaching.

### 3. Sales Pipelines

- **View Pipelines**: In the "Sales" section, you can see all the deals in various stages (Lead, Opportunity, Won).
- **Move Deals**: Use drag-and-drop to move deals between stages as they progress.

### 4. Send Emails

- **Send Email to Contact**: You can send emails directly from the CRM by selecting a contact and clicking "Send Email."
- **Use Email Templates**: Create and use email templates for faster email creation.

### 5. Reporting and Metrics

- **View Dashboard**: The "Dashboard" section shows a summary of key metrics, such as the number of tasks completed, deals won, and active contacts.

## Contact Support

If you have any issues or need help, please contact support at support@yourcrm.com.

## Conclusion

We hope this CRM helps you manage your customers and tasks more efficiently. Let us know if you have any feedback or suggestions!
